Cookie Policy

Last Updated: July 17, 2026
Current status: morningstaraigroup.com does not currently use analytics, advertising, or targeting cookies. We use only the minimum cookies necessary for the site to function. This policy is written to also cover categories we may introduce in the future, so it stays accurate as the site grows — you can manage your preferences for those categories in advance on our Cookie Preferences page.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your browser or device when you visit a website. They help a site remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to use the site and more useful to you.

Categories of Cookies

Strictly Necessary Always Active

Required for the website to function — such as remembering your cookie preferences, and, for our subscribers, keeping you securely signed in to Cadence or Bearing. These cannot be turned off, as the site or app would not work properly without them.

Performance & Analytics Not Currently In Use

Would help us understand how visitors use our site, such as which pages are most viewed, so we can improve it. Not currently active on morningstaraigroup.com.

Functional Not Currently In Use

Would remember preferences like display settings to give you a more personalized experience across visits. Not currently active on morningstaraigroup.com.

Targeting & Advertising Not Currently In Use

Would be used to show relevant advertising and measure marketing campaign performance across websites. Not currently active on morningstaraigroup.com.

Cookies Used by Cadence & Bearing

Once you're subscribed and logged in to Cadence or Bearing, the application itself uses strictly necessary cookies and similar local storage technology to keep you securely signed in and to remember your working preferences within the app. These are separate from the marketing website and are covered in more detail in your subscription agreement.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You can review and update your cookie preferences at any time on our Cookie Preferences page. You can also control cookies directly through your browser settings:

  • Microsoft Edge
  • Firefox
  • Google Chrome
  • Safari

Please note that disabling strictly necessary cookies may affect the functionality of the website and, for subscribers, your ability to stay signed in to Cadence or Bearing.

Changes to This Policy

If we introduce analytics, functional, or advertising cookies in the future, we'll update this policy and the categories available on our Cookie Preferences page accordingly.
